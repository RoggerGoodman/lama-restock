Manual Loss Recording

What This Does

Record product losses manually by uploading a CSV file:

  • ROTTURE - Broken or damaged products
  • SCADUTO - Expired products
  • UTILIZZO INTERNO - Internal usage (staff consumption, samples, etc.)
Important Notes
  • Stock will be automatically reduced by the loss amounts
  • File must be CSV format
  • Expected columns: Codice, Variante, Qta Originale
  • Products not in database will be skipped
  • Losses are tracked monthly in the database
