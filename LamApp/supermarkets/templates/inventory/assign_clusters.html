Assign Clusters to Products - PDF Format

How It Works
  1. Upload a PDF file exported from inventory terminal
  2. Enter a cluster name (e.g., FREEZER, DAIRY, PRODUCE)
  3. All products in the PDF are assigned to that cluster
  4. Stock quantities are ignored (only product codes matter)
PDF Format

Upload the same PDF format used for stock verification. The system will extract product codes and assign them to your chosen cluster.
