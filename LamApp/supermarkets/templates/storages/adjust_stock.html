When to Use This
  • Undelivered packages - Supplier didn't deliver an order
  • Extra deliveries - Received more than ordered
  • Damaged goods - Products damaged in transit
  • Inventory corrections - Fix database discrepancies
Important

This directly modifies the database stock level. Use positive numbers to add stock and negative numbers to remove stock. All adjustments are logged.
